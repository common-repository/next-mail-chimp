== Description ==

#### Next Mail for Subscribe and Campaigns

*Allowing your visitors to subscribe to your newsletter should be easy.* 

This plugin helps you grow your Mailchimp lists and write better newsletters through various methods. Select form design and setup mailchimp configration. You can create good looking opt-in forms or integrate with any existing form on your site, like your comment, contact or checkout form.
